Your Racism Fallacy Is:

  1. Other Races Are Not Intelligent Enough to Worry About
    Other races are not as intelligent and emotionally developed as my race, so killing them and causing them pain is acceptable.
  2. Other Races Attack Other Races, So I Can Too
    Because other countries are at war, there is nothing wrong with us attacking other countries, killing their citizens, and exploiting them.
  3. Being Racist Is My Personal Choice.
    Being racist is a personal choice, just as being vegan is a personal choice, so everyone should live and let live.
  4. Being Tolerant Is Too Difficult
    Being tolerant is too difficult and complicated for the average person, so it is unreasonable to expect everyone not to be racists.
  5. Interspecies Killing Is Natural
    Humans evolved as an expansionary species, so fighting with other races is natural for us.
  6. Being Tolerant Changes Nothing
    Not being racist changes nothing because there will always be other racism in the world, so what's the point?
  7. I Don't Have To Be Tolerant To Love People
    I love people enough to fulfill their needs while they're alive, but I also understand that sometimes they need to be killed to satisfy my needs. So there is no conflict between loving people and killing them.
  8. I Honor The Countries I Conquer
    I honor the countries I conquer during my expansions for giving me their territory.
  9. I Conquer Other Countries Only In A Humanitarian Way
    It's important for civilians to be treated well, so I only kill soldiers who went to war in a way that is free from suffering, and death is quick and humanitarian.
  10. My Race Is The Master Race
    Other races are genetically inferior to mine, so killing them and causing them pain is acceptable. From an evolutionary standpoint, only my race has the right to live.
  11. My Religion Allows Me To Kill Other People
    Religion is my moral compass, which gives me the right to kill people.
  12. Our Ancestors Conquered Other Countries
    Our ancestors have been conquering other countries and enslaving their citizens since the beginning of their existence, so it's good to continue this.
  13. Our Tradition Allows Or Mandates Killing Other People
    Because my culture or tradition allows or mandates it, morally I have a choice or I am obligated to kill people.
  14. Multiculturalism Is Brainwashing
    Raising children in multiculturalism is brainwashing because children are too young to make significant decisions on this topic. Children have the right to be racists, so forcing multiculturalism and tolerance on them is unethical.
  15. Multiculti Is Just A Passing Fad
    Multiculturalism is just another fad that will soon pass, like all the previous ones.
  16. Tolerant People Would Also Kill In War
    There are situations in which people who are not racists would kill another person if they had no other choice.
  17. It's Impossible To Be 100% Tolerant
    The philosophy of tolerance is flawed because tolerating every single person is impossible.
